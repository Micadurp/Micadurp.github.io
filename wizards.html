This was a school project at BTH where I worked with 14 other students to develop a game from the ground up including the game engine. I was in charge of the networking of the game and worked with one other person. It was written using using sockets and was a tremendous learning experience. A large chunk of the game was affected by the network and because of that I very often had to work with others and incorporate multiplayer code into their area. The project was around 5 months work and we had a mix of programmers and technical artists to develop it. We have no plans on finishing it but was fun to work on. The code is quite a mess and there are many things that need fixing but here is a link to the repository! Also note I am not the owner of the repository.
